Is AI The New Learner?

How the use of Artificial Intelligence negatively impacts the academic performance of students nowadays.

Recent developments in artificial intelligence platforms, such as Snapchat's "My AI" and ChapGPT, have had a significant impact on education. Although some would contend that artificial intelligence (AI) can be advantageous because it can instantly generate a flawless piece of writing, the repercussions are extremely severe. AI has caused significant problems in education that have affected how pupils learn. The absence of human interaction and reliance on AI are two detrimental effects of AI on education today.

Our Purpose

To help students like us find the sweet spot between using AI and learning the good old-fashioned way.

MISSION

To inspire students to trust their own skills and abilities instead of relying on AI tools.

VISION

A future where students learn, grow, and achieve success through their own efforts, without depending on artificial intelligence.

OUR OBJECTIVES

Promote Awareness

Educate students about the potential negative impacts of over-reliance on AI, such as loss of critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills.

Provide an Alternative

Offer students practical, non-AI-based tools and strategies to complete tasks, such as using physical books, brainstorming techniques, and peer discussions.

Foster Self-Reliance

Motivate students to complete schoolwork and projects independently, reinforcing confidence in their own abilities.

Encourage independent learning.

Advocate for traditional learning methods that build deeper understanding and academic integrity without the help of AI tools.

Minimizing AI in Learning: The Power of Doing It Yourself

AI is here, and it’s changing how we learn, but are we letting it do too much? While AI tools can help with certain tasks, it’s crucial that we don’t rely on them for everything. The key is to minimize AI use and keep learning the old-fashioned way by thinking, creating, and solving problems on our own.

Why Minimizing AI is Essential for Our Growth

Critical Thinking Needs Space to Grow

Relying too much on AI can make us lazy thinkers. When we minimize AI, we give ourselves the space to think through problems and develop stronger critical thinking skills.

Creativity Comes from Effort

AI can solve problems, but it can’t think creatively like we can. Minimizing AI gives us the opportunity to innovate and develop unique ideas.

Real Learning Happens Through Practice

True learning comes from effort and practice. When we minimize AI use, we push ourselves to learn, make mistakes, and improve. The more we do this, the more we grow. AI shouldn’t replace the effort needed to truly understand a subject.

AI Should Be a Tool, Not a Shortcut

AI can be helpful when used wisely, but it should never replace the learning process. By minimizing its role, we ensure that we’re still developing the skills we need, instead of relying on AI to do everything for us.
